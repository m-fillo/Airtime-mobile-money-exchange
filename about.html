About Our Company

Leading the digital financial revolution in Sierra Leone.

Who We Are

Founded in 2022, SL Money Exchange was born out of a simple need: to make mobile money transfers between different networks in Sierra Leone seamless, fast, and affordable. We recognized the challenges people faced when trying to move funds between Orange, Afri, and QMoney, and we built a solution to bridge that gap.

Today, we are proud to be the most trusted independent mobile money hub in the country, serving thousands of Sierra Leoneans daily. Our platform is built on a foundation of transparency, security, and customer-first service.

Our Mission

To empower every Sierra Leonean with the ability to move their digital funds freely across all networks, fostering financial inclusion and economic growth through innovative technology.

Our Vision

To become the leading digital financial ecosystem in West Africa, where mobile money is as fluid and accessible as physical cash, regardless of the service provider.
